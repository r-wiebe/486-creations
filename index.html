Questions?

Is it safe for kids? What's the finish?

The butcher block top is finished with Rubio Monocoat — a tough, food-safe hardwax oil that high-end furniture makers use. It wipes clean easily and holds up to daily use, spills, and everything else kids bring to the table.

Does it arrive assembled?

It ships flat-packed to keep shipping costs down. Assembly is straightforward and only needs basic hand tools.

How big is it? Will it fit my kids?

The table is 22" × 32" and stands 17" tall; each bench is 9" × 32" and 9.5" tall. We list the full dimensions in the product details above so you can check the fit for your space and your kids before you order.
